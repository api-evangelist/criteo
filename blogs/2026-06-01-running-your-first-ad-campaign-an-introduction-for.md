Launching a first advertising campaign is often treated as a technical milestone. Business owners spend weeks researching platforms, comparing targeting options, reading articles about bidding strategies, and trying to understand which channel will deliver the best return. The assumption is that successful advertising is primarily a matter of mastering the tools.
